Welcome to word game wonders!

The rules are as follows:
    • A random word of seven letters or more will be generated.
      As the word appears a timer will begin and it's a race against the clock.
      The object of the game is to create seven words of 3 letters or more.
      Seperate each word with a space when entering them into the text box.
      As soon as you hit 'Done' the timer will stop and you will be told how many you got correct!
      Get all seven correct and you are a winner!
      Get in the Top 10 times to add yourself to the leaderboard!
  • Good luck!
